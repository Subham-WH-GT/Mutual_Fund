1. Overview of the Project

This project leverages machine learning to predict the best-fit mutual fund scheme based on user input data. It analyzes various parameters such as financial goals, risk tolerance, and investment horizon to provide personalized recommendations.

2. Purpose and Goal

The purpose of this project is to assist users in making informed investment decisions by providing tailored mutual fund recommendations. Our goal is to enhance user understanding of mutual fund options and improve investment outcomes based on individual preferences.

User Input Section

To predict the best-fit mutual fund scheme, please provide the following information:

  • Min SIP: Minimum amount for monthly investments in the mutual fund.
  • Min Lumpsum: Minimum one-time investment required for the mutual fund.
  • Expense Ratio: Annual fee as a percentage of the fundâ€™s total assets, indicating the cost of managing the fund.
  • Alpha: Measures the fund's active return compared to its benchmark index, indicating the manager's performance.
  • Beta: Indicates the fund's volatility relative to the market, helping assess risk.
  • Sharpe Ratio: Risk-adjusted return metric, indicating how well the return compensates for the risk taken.
  • Risk Level: Numeric representation of the mutual fund's risk, rated from 1 (low) to 5 (high).
  • Returns 1 Year (%): Percentage return generated by the fund over the past year.
  • Returns 3 Year (%): Percentage return over the past three years.
  • Returns 5 Year (%): Percentage return over the past five years.
  • Category: Select the mutual fund category (e.g., Equity, Hybrid).

Once you fill in the above details, click on the "Predict" button to receive personalized mutual fund recommendations.
